The Best Movies According to Xai

My top 3 movies of all time.

The Lord of the Rings

Wonderful fantasy movie that brought to life one of the most amazing worlds in literature.

The Pirates of the Carribean

Exciting, action-packed, and witty series of swash-buckling movies. Except for the 7th installment. We don't talk about that.

Godfather 1 and 2

Excellent storytelling with rich characters. We don't talk about Godfather 3. Don't even watch Godfather 3.
